Why we exist

Built from
a scattered scene

The UAE adventure scene was fragmented — solo riders, scattered groups, no structure. GOT brought it together: rides by skill level, real training, and one app to run it all.

  • Rides matched to your assessed skill level
  • Structured progression, not just meetups
  • Exclusive trainings with local and international renowned trainers
  • EMSO-sanctioned events
